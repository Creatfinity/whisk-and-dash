Privacy Policy

Welcome to Chirpy! We provide you with daily articles and fun quizzes to keep you engaged and informed. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to enhance your experience with our app:

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and any other details you provide.
  • Usage Data: We gather information on how you interact with the app, including articles read, quizzes taken, and your engagement levels.
  • Device Information: We may collect data about your device, such as its type, operating system, and unique identifiers.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we may track your location to provide localized content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services:

  • Enhance User Experience: Tailor content and quizzes based on your interests and preferences.
  • Communication: Send you updates, notifications, and personalized recommendations.
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage data to enhance app functionality and user engagement.
  • Security: Monitor for fraudulent activities and protect user accounts.
  • Compliance: Ensure adherence to legal requirements and protect the rights of all users.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in specific situations:

  • With Third-Party Services: We may share information with partners that help us operate and improve our app.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose information to comply with laws, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights.
  • Business Transactions: If our business is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you give us permission.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to or correction of your information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of promotional communications at any time.

Contact us to exercise these rights as detailed in the "Contact Us" section.

6. Children's Privacy

Chirpy is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If we become aware that we have done so, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be posted in the app, and continued use of the app signifies acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us at creatfinity@gmail.com.
